Pimengo

Caren TIVA

Pimengo is a service delivered by LimonetiK, a young company founded in 2007 by 5 very talented guys in Paris. Since one of their area of expertise is Internet payments, they developed Pimengo: a new kind of gift certificate that is valid on the most famous French websites. The goal was to develop a playful, fresh brand and website and we were pretty happy with the results.
